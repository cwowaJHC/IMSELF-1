Briefly — how it will work (scenario)

The user logs in → sees the search bar “Describe your problem” (natural language) or selects a category/subcategory. The search recognizes the problem → creates a “PROBLEM” card (tab). Tabs are like in a browser: you can switch, drag, and pin them. The main tab shows: recommended practices (at the beginning), books, videos, applications, and a plan (AI planner).
